Set along the banks of Lake Creek on a quiet loop street — the kind where children still play outside and neighbors know each other by name — this home offers something increasingly rare in Central Texas: genuine tranquility, steps from everything.

Live oak canopy lines the street. Manicured lawns and well-kept homes signal a neighborhood that takes pride in itself. And behind this home, the creek provides a private, ever-changing natural backdrop that most buyers can only dream about.

The thoughtfully designed floor plan maximizes every square foot, and the 28’ × 17’ covered deck extends your living space into one of the finest back yards you’ll find in this zip code.
