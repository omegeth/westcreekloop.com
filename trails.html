The neighborhood around 1603 West Creek Loop is woven together by trails, greenbelts, and a new city-funded trail connection that puts downtown Round Rock within walking distance — without touching a car.

On Your Doorstep

The Trails of West Creek Loop

~1 Mile · Paved
Lake Creek Trail to Downtown New
The city of Round Rock's newly completed Lake Creek Trail runs from Round Rock West Park, through a pedestrian underpass beneath I-35, across a new bridge over Lake Creek, and connects to Centennial Plaza in the heart of downtown. Walk to the farmers market, arts festivals, restaurants, and community events — no car required.
🚶 Walking 🚲 Biking Paved Downtown Access City of Round Rock
Short Walk · Neighborhood
Shadow Brook Pool & Tennis
A short, pleasant walk from the house to the private Shadow Brook Pool and tennis courts. Card-access only — exclusively for Shadow Brook subdivision residents. Perfect for morning laps or an afternoon match.
🏊 Pool 🎾 Tennis Private Access Residents Only
Walkable · All Ages
Schools in the Neighborhood
Elementary, middle, and high school are all within the neighborhood — kids walk to school on quiet residential streets through a mature, tree-lined community. No bus route required, no school-traffic gauntlet.
🎒 Walkable Schools Family-Friendly Safe Streets
Extensive · Natural Surface
Round Rock West Greenbelt
The Round Rock West Greenbelt follows Lake Creek through the neighborhood, connecting open green space, wildlife habitat, and informal trail access along the creek corridor. A natural extension of your backyard.
🌿 Greenbelt Wildlife Creek Access Dog-Friendly
Nearby · Paved Loops
Round Rock West Park
Round Rock West Park sits at the trailhead of the new Lake Creek Trail. Paved paths, open green space, pond, and a natural staging point for longer walks toward downtown. The park is a hub connecting multiple trail directions.
🏞️ Park Paved Paths Trail Hub Pond
Neighborhood Loop
West Creek Loop Itself
The loop street is its own amenity. No through traffic means a safe, quiet route for morning walks with the dog, evening strolls with kids, or just wandering past neighbors' gardens under a canopy of live oaks that took decades to grow.
🐕 Dog Walk Loop Street No Through Traffic Live Oaks

Lake Creek Trail: Neighborhood to Downtown

The red line traces the approximately one-mile Lake Creek Trail route — from the Round Rock West neighborhood (where West Creek Loop sits, bottom left), along the creek, through a new pedestrian underpass beneath I-35, across the new Lake Creek bridge, and into Centennial Plaza in downtown Round Rock. Sprouts Farmers Market, restaurants, and annual events like the Round Rock Arts Fest are now a walk away.

Community Impact · October 2025
Bridge Placed Over Lake Creek to Connect Round Rock West with Downtown
The City of Round Rock used a crane to place a new bridge over Lake Creek as part of the one-mile Lake Creek Trail project. The trail connects Round Rock West Park through a pedestrian underpass under I-35 to Centennial Plaza downtown. The $2.46M project was approved by voters in 2023 and was completed in late 2025 / early 2026.
